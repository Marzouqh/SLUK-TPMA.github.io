Teaching Practice Management Application

NITDA in collaboration with Sule Lamido University Kafin Hausa is vissionned to automate the Students Teaching Practice Program

The Aims of the Teaching Practice Management Application

The TPMA project is designed to overcome the challenges of monitoring and guiding teaching practice students, particularly those caused by distance constraints, inadequate staffing, and other logistical difficulties. To address these issues, TPMA aims to:

  • Supervisor Dashboard to monitor the progress and performance of assigned teacher-trainees.
  • Teacher-Trainee Profile for tracking their personal progress and submitting work.
  • Admin Panel for university administrators to manage users, assign supervisors, and monitor overall system activity.
