Sule Lamido University TPMA Project

The Teaching Practice Management Application (TPMA) at Sule Lamido University is designed to streamline and enhance the supervision, monitoring, and evaluation of student-teachers during their teaching practice. The platform leverages digital technology to improve efficiency, ensure real-time communication between supervisors and student-teachers, and provide a structured framework for tracking progress.

This initiative aligns with the vision of the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA), which aims to drive digital transformation in Nigeria's education sector. By integrating technology into teacher education, TPMA supports NITDA's mission to promote innovation, digital literacy, and efficient service delivery through ICT.
